  Malenadu Gidda (Malnad Gidda) is a small indigenous breed from the hilly Western Ghats belt.
  Despite its dwarf frame, this breed is known for intelligence, calm behavior, and adaptability

  - Small body frame, usually around 2-3 feet in height
  - Suited to high-rainfall, herbal-rich hill ecosystems
  - Traditionally used in temple rituals and abhisheka seva
